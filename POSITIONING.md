# OliveroDev — Positioning Strategy

## Primary audience

Established small and mid-sized businesses whose current website is slow, vulnerable, difficult to maintain, or failing to generate qualified opportunities.

## Positioning statement

OliveroDev turns underperforming business websites into fast, secure, maintainable platforms designed to earn trust and generate qualified opportunities.

## Differentiators

- Direct access to the senior developer doing the work.
- More than 10 years across government, healthcare, EdTech, and enterprise projects.
- One technical partner for performance, security, SEO foundations, migration, and implementation.
- Clear scope, measurable before/after evidence, and launch support.

## Offer architecture

1. **Website Audit & Action Plan** — a low-risk entry offer that identifies performance, security, SEO, UX, and maintenance priorities.
2. **Optimize & Protect** — performance optimization, security hardening, and recovery for existing websites.
3. **Rebuild & Migrate** — modernize outdated platforms without losing content or SEO equity.
4. **New Website or MVP** — a secondary offer for qualified businesses that need a new platform.
5. **Ongoing Technical Partnership** — post-launch monitoring, improvements, and senior technical support.

## Message hierarchy

1. Business outcome: a website that earns trust and supports growth.
2. Current pain: slow, vulnerable, hard to update, or not converting.
3. Evidence: relevant projects, exact responsibilities, and verified before/after results.
4. Reduced risk: direct senior involvement, clear process, measurable scope, and launch support.
5. Next step: request an audit or book a strategy call.

## Messaging guardrails

- Lead with business problems and outcomes; technology supports the story.
- Never publish a metric without a source, time period, and clear ownership.
- Avoid guarantees and absolute claims unless they are contractually supportable.
- Keep MVPs and plugins secondary so they do not dilute the main service position.
